Chocolate Chip Banana Bread

Description

This delicious recipe is perfect for those who are craving a warm, moist dessert or breakfast (or both!) recipe. This delicious bread is loaded with ripe bananas and melty chocolate chips to create a mouth-watering experience.

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1 tablespoon milk
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 cup butter, softened

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips
